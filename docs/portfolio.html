I deployed all over southern Europe, and I was able to gain tremendous world perspective at a young age. After my stint in Army, I helped manage, and eventually owned a portion of, Watkins Boxing & Muay Thai in Indiana, PA. I managed all the digital content, the financials, and helped train professional & amateur fighters. Now I attend Penn State as an adult learner, and I couldn’t be happier.
